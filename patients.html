Before Your Visit

Review guidelines and documents required for admissions and day procedures.

Admissions

  • Carry government-issued ID & insurance documents
  • Pre-authorization assistance for cashless treatment
  • Dedicated welcome lounges at every hospital

Discharge Process

  • Discharge summary & prescriptions explained in detail
  • Billing transparency with itemized invoices
  • Post-discharge follow-up scheduled before exit

Visitor Guidelines

  • Visiting hours: 10 AM – 7 PM (varies by unit)
  • Two visitors permitted at a time
  • Mask and hygiene protocols mandatory

Patient Rights & Responsibilities

We promote respectful partnerships between patients, families, and care teams.

Your Rights

  • Receive clear, respectful communication
  • Participate in decisions about your care
  • Access your medical records securely

Your Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate health and insurance information
  • Respect hospital policies and staff guidance
  • Adhere to treatment plans and follow-up schedules
